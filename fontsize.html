Breed History

The briard which is believed to have originated from france, has bred for centuries to
and to protect sheep . the breed was used bt the french army as sentries,
messangers and to find wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing.
The briards were used in the first world war in the point of extinction.Currently
