The briards , the or the berger de brie is alarge breed of dog traditionally used for herding large number of sheep

Breed history

The Briard is an ancient breed of large herding dog, originally from France. The breed became popular after the Paris dog show of 1863, after having been fixed with crosses with the Beauceron and the Barbet.
